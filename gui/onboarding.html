An API key connects QuickSay to Groq's speech engine. Free, takes just a few minutes, and your audio is never stored.

  1. Go to console.groq.com/keys and sign up (Google, GitHub, or email)
  2. Hit Create API Key — name it anything
  3. Copy the key (starts with gsk_...) and paste below

What QuickSay Does

A quick look before we walk you through the settings.

Voice to Text
Hold Ctrl + Win, speak, release. Text appears wherever your cursor is. You can also click the floating widget to start recording — no keyboard needed.
Smart Modes
Standard, Email, Code, Casual. Each mode cleans up your text differently.
Stats and History
Word counts, streaks, full transcription history. All tracked automatically.
Fully Customizable
Hotkeys, dictionary, sound themes, import/export. Make it yours.
Sticky Mode
Prefer tap-to-talk? Enable Sticky Mode in Settings for hands-free dictation — tap to start, tap again to stop.

You'll explore all of these in the settings tour next.
